Podium Multiplayer

Podium provides an immersive and competitive racing experience, allowing you to pit your skills against real-world opponents and rise through the ranks to become an F1 legend.

Play against friends

Play with players around the world, your friend list or even with those around you by using room codes.

Multiplayer mode uses a matchmaking system to pair players with similar skill levels, ensuring fair and competitive races.

Choose from a wide range of race formats, such as quick races, custom tournaments, and the grand prix seasons.

Rewards

Participating in multiplayer races earns you rewards like in-game currency, experience points (xp), and exclusive items.

Advance through the divisions and tiers to discover new challenges and opportunities.

Earn achievements and trophies by completing specific objectives, mastering game mechanics, and skilful driving.

Meet your match

PODIUM uses a matchmaking system to pair players with similar skill levels, resulting in fair and competitive races.

Form alliances, share strategies, and compete together in multiplayer events.

Track your progress with the leaderboard and compare your performance against others.
