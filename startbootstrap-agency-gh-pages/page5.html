Frequently Asked Questions


How do I purchase PODIUM?

For digital downloads, applicable consoles all versions of the game can be found via your gaming device app store. Physical versions will be found in selected gaming stores. For Windows and MacOS digital copies can be downloaded via app store and Steam.

What happens if I lose internet connection?

If internet connection is lost, your screen will be met with a loading page ready to get you back on the track! The game will pause for any other players driving with you so you will not face any unfair disadvantages to your place.

Can I play offline?

Yes play offline in Practice mode on every device including mobile gaming and perfect your driving talent.

Can I use voucher/discount codes?

Yes, we accept voucher and discount codes so look out for any exclusive rare offers!
