Management

Manage your own Formula 1 team

Become a manager for one of any legendary F1 Team and see the ins and outs of being a manager.


Team management

Hire and fire any drivers, engineers, and other staff members.

Manage the team's finances, including sponsorships and contracts.

Upgrade facilities, making your grid lineup unbeatable.

Race weekends

Determine race strategies such as tire selection, fuel level, pit stop timing, and driver aggressiveness.

Qualification for grid positions is based on performance in qualifying sessions.

Adapt strategies based on weather conditions, track characteristics, and competitor performance.

Driver management

Negotiate contracts with drivers. Scout and recruit talented drivers and engineer from around the world.

Monitor and manage driver morale, fitness levels, and skill development.

Handle driver rivalry and team dynamics, which can have an impact on team cohesion and performance.

League management

Compete in a dynamic league system that includes several seasons, championships, and divisions.

Manage team progression by promoting and relegating players depending on performance.

Participate in unique events like demonstration races, endurance races, and historic races.
